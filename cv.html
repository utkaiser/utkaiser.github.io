Research Area

I am broadly interested in robotics, generative modeling and applied mathematics.
My long-term goals are to i) design novel reinforcement learning algorithms that use general purpose foundation models (language, vision or multimodal), and ii) ground deep learning in mathematical principles through partial differential equations.
