Research Area

To make sense of the world using data, I combine methods from numerical analysis, deep learning and partial differential equations. My long-term goal is to 1) develop novel training algorithms to enhance numerical solvers with deep learning (e.g., wave propagation) 2) apply deep reinforcement learning and Monte Carlo tree search strategies to game theory problems (e.g., hide-and-seek games).
